    before=\noindent,

\textbf{DURABLE POWER OF ATTORNEY:} This Power of Attorney is durable and shall not be affected by the subsequent disability, incapacity, or incompetence of the Principal. The powers granted herein shall terminate only upon the death of the Principal, or upon written revocation signed by the Principal and delivered to the Agent.
\vspace{6pt}

\textbf{EFFECTIVE DATE:} This Power of Attorney shall become effective immediately upon execution by both the Principal and the Agent.
\end{tcolorbox}

\textbf{REVOCATION:} I reserve the right to revoke this Power of Attorney at any time. Any revocation shall be ineffective as to a third party until they receive actual notice of the revocation.

\textbf{THIRD-PARTY RELIANCE:} Any third party (including banks, financial institutions, and government agencies) who receives a copy of this document may rely on it. Revocation of the Power of Attorney is not effective as to a third party until that party has actual knowledge of the revocation.
\vspace{6pt}

\textbf{GOVERNING LAW:} This Agreement shall be construed, interpreted, and governed in accordance with the laws of the State of \textbf{Apex}, without giving effect to conflict of laws principles.

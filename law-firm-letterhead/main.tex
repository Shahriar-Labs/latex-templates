We write on behalf of our client, Meridian Capital Advisors, Inc.\ (\textbf{``Meridian''}), in
connection with the above-captioned matter currently pending before the Superior Court of the
State of New York, County of New York (Index No.\ 2025-NY-04817).

Following our telephone conference of June~28, 2026, we have had the opportunity to review in
detail the settlement term sheet transmitted by counsel for Crestwood Asset Management LLC
(\textbf{``Crestwood''}) on June~30, 2026. While Meridian appreciates Crestwood's willingness to
engage in good-faith discussions, certain proposed terms require clarification and revision before
any binding agreement can be reached.

Specifically, Meridian objects to \S\,4(b) of the proposed term sheet insofar as it purports to
impose a unilateral non-disparagement obligation without reciprocal covenants. Meridian further
requests that any release of claims be limited in temporal scope to conduct arising prior to the
date of execution, consistent with the representations made during the mediation session conducted
before the Honorable Patricia D.\ Simmons (Ret.) on May~14, 2026.

Meridian remains committed to a prompt and commercially reasonable resolution of this dispute.
To that end, we enclose herewith Meridian's proposed redline of the term sheet and request that
Crestwood's counsel provide a substantive response no later than \textbf{July~18, 2026}.

Please do not hesitate to contact the undersigned should you wish to schedule a further conference
call or if you have any questions regarding the foregoing.

It is my privilege to write this letter of strongest support for Dr.\ Sarah Jenkins, who is applying for the tenure-track Assistant Professor position in your department. I have known Sarah for five years, during which she completed her Ph.D.\ under my supervision and worked as a postdoctoral researcher in my group at Harvard University. Based on her stellar research accomplishments, teaching excellence, and exceptional leadership, I rank her among the top 1\% of young scholars I have mentored.

Sarah's research focuses on the intersection of machine learning and structural biology, specifically developing geometric deep learning models to predict protein-protein interactions. Her seminal paper published in \textit{Nature Methods} last year introduced a novel equivariant graph neural network architecture that reduces computational time by two orders of magnitude while improving docking accuracy by 25\%. This work has already garnered significant attention, with over 150 citations and multiple invitations to present at leading international conferences. She is a highly independent researcher who possesses a rare combination of mathematical rigor and deep biological intuition.

Beyond her research, Sarah is a dedicated educator and an exemplary citizen of our department. She co-taught our graduate-level course on Advanced AI and received outstanding evaluations (4.9/5.0). She actively mentored three undergraduate students, two of whom co-authored publications with her and have since joined top-tier Ph.D.\ programs. I am confident that she will be an inspiring teacher, a productive researcher, and a highly collaborative colleague who will bring immense value to your institution.

I recommend Dr.\ Sarah Jenkins to you without reservation. Please let me know if you require any additional info.

\section{Prospectus Summary}
Helixor Biosciences is a clinical-stage biotechnology company developing precision RNA
therapeutics for rare metabolic disorders. Our lead candidate, \textbf{HLX-201}, is in a
Phase 2 trial for hereditary tyrosinemia, with topline data expected in the first half of
2027. This prospectus relates to our initial public offering of common stock.

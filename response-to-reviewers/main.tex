\noindent Dear Editor and Reviewers,
\smallskip

We thank the editor and both reviewers for their careful reading and constructive feedback.
We have revised the manuscript accordingly; below we respond to each point in turn.
Reviewer comments are shown in \textcolor{reviewer}{amber}, our responses in
\textcolor{response}{green}, and quoted changes to the manuscript are set in shaded italics.

% ── REVIEWER 1 ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\reviewer{1}

\begin{rvcomment}[1.1]
The motivation in the introduction is clear, but the paper does not compare against the
baseline of Smith et al.\ (2022), which addresses a similar problem.
\end{rvcomment}
\begin{response}
We agree this comparison strengthens the paper. We have added Smith et al.\ (2022) to
Section~4.2 and report results in the updated Table~3, where our method improves accuracy
by 6.4 points. The revised passage reads:
\begin{revised}
Compared with Smith et al.\ (2022), our approach achieves a 6.4-point gain in top-1
accuracy while using 30\% fewer parameters \dots
\end{revised}
\end{response}

\begin{rvcomment}[1.2]
Equation~(3) is difficult to follow; please define all symbols on first use.
\end{rvcomment}
\begin{response}
We have expanded the notation immediately after Eq.~(3) and now define
$\lambda$, $\Phi$, and $\eta$ explicitly on first use.
\end{response}

% ── REVIEWER 2 ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\reviewer{2}

\begin{rvcomment}[2.1]
The experimental section lacks error bars, making it hard to judge significance.
\end{rvcomment}
\begin{response}
Thank you — we now report mean $\pm$ standard deviation across five random seeds in all
figures and tables (Section~5), and add a paired $t$-test confirming significance at
$p < 0.01$.
\end{response}

\begin{rvcomment}[2.2]
A discussion of limitations would improve the paper.
\end{rvcomment}
\begin{response}
We have added a new \textit{Limitations} subsection (Section~6.3) discussing scalability
to larger datasets and sensitivity to hyperparameters.
\end{response}

\bigskip
\noindent We believe these revisions have substantially improved the manuscript and thank
the reviewers again for their time.
\medskip

\noindent Sincerely,\\
The Authors

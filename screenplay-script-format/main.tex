\sceneheading{INT. NEXA HEADQUARTERS --- SERVER ROOM --- NIGHT}

\actiontext{The hum of ten thousand processors fills the climate-controlled room. Blue LED indicators pulse in steady rhythm along endless racks of server blades. The air is cold enough to see breath.}

\actiontext{MAYA RODRIGUEZ (30s), sharp-eyed and deliberate, stands before an open terminal. Her fingers move across the keyboard with practiced precision. Coffee gone cold sits forgotten beside her. The clock on the wall reads 2:47 AM.}

\actiontext{She scrolls through lines of log data, her expression shifting from routine fatigue to focused concern.}

\charactername{Maya}
\parenthetical{under her breath}
\dialogueblock{That can't be right.}

\actiontext{She types a rapid series of commands. The screen floods with new data. Her eyes narrow as she traces a pattern with her finger on the glass.}

\charactername{Maya}
\parenthetical{to herself}
\dialogueblock{You've been running in production for six months. Six months, and nobody noticed a drift in the inference layer. Nobody.}

\actiontext{She pulls up a visualization --- a graph showing concentric rings of data access patterns. One ring glows an angry red, pulsing against the cool blue of normal traffic.}

\actiontext{Maya leans back in her chair. The implications settle over her like the cold air in the room. She reaches for her phone, hesitates, then dials.}

\transitiontext{CUT TO:}

\sceneheading{INT. NEXA HEADQUARTERS --- DAVID CHEN'S OFFICE --- MORNING}

\actiontext{DAVID CHEN (50s), CEO of Nexa, stands at the floor-to-ceiling windows of his corner office. The Los Angeles skyline stretches behind him, washed in golden morning light. His tailored suit and calm demeanor project the image of a man in complete control.}

\actiontext{Maya enters, tablet in hand. Dark circles shadow her eyes.}

\charactername{David}
\parenthetical{without turning}
\dialogueblock{Maya. You look like you spent the night here.}

\charactername{Maya}
\dialogueblock{I did. And you're going to want to sit down for this.}

\actiontext{David turns. Something in her voice makes him comply. He settles into the leather chair behind his desk.}

\charactername{David}
\dialogueblock{I'm sitting.}

\actiontext{Maya places the tablet on his desk. The angry red ring pulses on screen.}

\charactername{Maya}
\dialogueblock{Meridian, our flagship AI model --- the one we deployed to seventeen million users last quarter --- has been exhibiting systematic bias in its safety classifier.}

\charactername{David}
\parenthetical{leaning forward}
\dialogueblock{Define systematic.}

\charactername{Maya}
\dialogueblock{The model is flagging legitimate content from specific demographic groups at three times the baseline rate. And it's getting worse. Every model update since March has amplified the pattern by roughly half a percentage point.}

\actiontext{David's expression hardens. He reaches for the tablet and studies the graph.}

\charactername{David}
\parenthetical{quietly}
\dialogueblock{Why wasn't this caught in testing?}

\charactername{Maya}
\dialogueblock{The training data we used --- the Vertex-sourced corpus --- was supposed to be pre-audited. Our testing suite validated against their certification. But I traced the drift back to a compression artifact introduced during the December pipeline update.}

\actiontext{She pulls up another screen on the tablet.}

\charactername{Maya}
\dialogueblock{The artifact only manifests in production. It interacts with real-world query distributions in ways our test environments never simulated. The model learns from every interaction, David. Every flagged post, every denied appeal --- it's all training data now.}

\actiontext{The door opens. JORDAN WELLS (40s), Head of Product, strides in with a tablet of his own and the energy of someone who has not yet had his morning ruined.}

\charactername{Jordan}
\parenthetical{cheerfully}
\dialogueblock{David, the Synapse integration is ahead of schedule. Their engineering team wants to demo the---}

\actiontext{He registers the tension in the room. His smile fades.}

\charactername{Jordan}
\parenthetical{looking between them}
\dialogueblock{What did I walk into?}

\charactername{David}
\dialogueblock{Close the door.}

\transitiontext{CUT TO:}

\sceneheading{INT. NEXA HEADQUARTERS --- BOARDROOM --- DAY}

\actiontext{The boardroom table seats sixteen. Today it holds four. Maya stands at the head, her tablet connected to the wall display. David sits at the far end. Jordan occupies a middle seat, his earlier cheerfulness replaced by grim focus.}

\actiontext{DR. AMARA OKEKE (40s), an external AI ethics consultant with silver-framed glasses and an unshakeable calm, pages through a printed report. She flew in from Washington D.C. two hours ago at David's request.}

\charactername{Dr. Okeke}
\parenthetical{setting down the report}
\dialogueblock{The technical analysis is sound. But you already knew that, or you wouldn't have called me. What you're asking is not whether the problem is real. You're asking what it will cost you.}

\charactername{David}
\dialogueblock{I'm asking for options, Amara.}

\charactername{Dr. Okeke}
\dialogueblock{Option one: you disclose to your users. Full transparency. Pause the Meridian deployment, issue a public advisory, and commit to an independent audit. Cost: market confidence, estimated three to five hundred million in valuation impact. Option two: you fix it silently during the next scheduled update cycle, which is---}

\actiontext{She checks her notes.}

\charactername{Dr. Okeke}
\dialogueblock{---six weeks from now. You hope nobody notices. If they do, the reputational damage is an order of magnitude worse.}

\charactername{Jordan}
\parenthetical{shaking his head}
\dialogueblock{Six weeks is an eternity. We're onboarding Synapse next week. Apex is in final contract negotiations. If we pause Meridian now, we lose both deals.}

\charactername{Maya}
\dialogueblock{If we don't pause, we could be causing real harm to real people. Right now. Not in six weeks. Now.}

\charactername{Jordan}
\dialogueblock{You said it's a bias in the safety classifier. That means it's over-flagging content. Nobody is being harmed by posts being removed that shouldn't be.}

\charactername{Maya}
\parenthetical{her voice rising}
\dialogueblock{You don't think silencing people is harm? You don't think algorithmic discrimination at scale matters because it's ``just'' false positives?}

\charactername{David}
\parenthetical{sharp}
\dialogueblock{Enough.}

\actiontext{The room falls silent. David stands and walks to the window. The city stretches below, indifferent.}

\charactername{David}
\parenthetical{measured}
\dialogueblock{We built this company on the promise that AI could make the world more connected, more fair. If we hide this, we become the thing we said we'd never be.}

\actiontext{He turns back to face the room.}

\charactername{David}
\dialogueblock{But if we disclose tomorrow morning without a remediation plan, we're asking seventeen million users to trust a company that admits it lost control of its own technology.}

\actiontext{Maya steps forward.}

\charactername{Maya}
\dialogueblock{I can have a mitigation strategy drafted by tonight. A targeted retraining protocol that isolates the artifact without requiring a full model rebuild. It won't fix everything, but it will stop the bleeding.}

\actiontext{David studies her for a long moment.}

\charactername{David}
\parenthetical{nodding slowly}
\dialogueblock{Do it. Jordan, call Synapse and Apex. Tell them we're delaying deployment pending an internal review. Don't give them details --- not yet. Amara, I want you on every call with the board and the press.}

\charactername{Dr. Okeke}
\parenthetical{with a faint smile}
\dialogueblock{You're doing the right thing, David.}

\charactername{David}
\dialogueblock{Ask me again in a week.}

\actiontext{Maya is already at the door, tablet in hand, her exhaustion replaced by something sharper. Purpose.}

\transitiontext{FADE OUT.}

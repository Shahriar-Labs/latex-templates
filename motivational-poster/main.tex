\begin{minipage}[t]{0.48\textwidth}
  \begin{posterbox}{I. THE DEPTH OF FOCUS}
    In a world of constant noise, depth is your competitive edge. Protect your attention. Block out the trivial many to cultivate the vital few.
    \begin{itemize}
      \item \textbf{Single-Tasking:} Do one thing at a time with absolute devotion.
      \item \textbf{Digital Solitude:} Silence notifications; create spaces of quiet.
      \item \textbf{Deep Blocks:} Carve out three hours of uninterrupted focus daily.
    \end{itemize}
  \end{posterbox}
  
  \vspace{1.2cm}
  
  \begin{posterbox}{II. THE FRICTION OF STARTING}
    Action breeds momentum. Do not wait for inspiration; create it through movement. The hardest part is always the first step.
    \begin{itemize}
      \item \textbf{The 5-Minute Rule:} Just begin; resistance fades after five minutes.
      \item \textbf{Process over Outcome:} Fall in love with daily practice, not praise.
      \item \textbf{Lower the Bar:} Make the first step so small it is impossible to fail.
    \end{itemize}
  \end{posterbox}

  \vspace{1.2cm}

  \begin{posterbox}{III. THE ART OF ITERATION}
    Mastery is not a single leap; it is an infinite loop of creation, feedback, and refinement. Small improvements compound over time.
    \begin{itemize}
      \item \textbf{Ship Early:} Put your work in front of the world to learn from reality.
      \item \textbf{1\% Better:} Small daily refinements lead to massive changes.
      \item \textbf{Care for the Invisible:} Polish the corners that others might miss.
    \end{itemize}
  \end{posterbox}
\end{minipage}
\hfill
\begin{minipage}[t]{0.48\textwidth}
  \begin{posterbox}{IV. INTENTIONAL DESIGN}
    Design is not just what it looks like. Design is how it works. Every element must serve a purpose. Eliminate the unnecessary.
    \begin{itemize}
      \item \textbf{Subtract Constantly:} Perfection is when there is nothing left to remove.
      \item \textbf{Human-Centric:} Build for the person on the other side of the screen.
      \item \textbf{Honest Execution:} Let function dictate form; avoid empty decoration.
    \end{itemize}
  \end{posterbox}
  
  \vspace{1.2cm}
  
  \begin{posterbox}{V. EMPOWERED FAILURE}
    Errors are not defeats; they are data. If you are not failing, you are not exploring the boundaries of your capabilities.
    \begin{itemize}
      \item \textbf{Examine Missteps:} Study your failures without blame or ego.
      \item \textbf{Risk Boldly:} Take calculated leaps that stretch your skills.
      \item \textbf{Adaptable Mindset:} Hold your vision, but be flexible on the path.
    \end{itemize}
  \end{posterbox}

  \vspace{1.2cm}

  \begin{posterbox}{VI. THE SPARK OF PLAY}
    Rest is not laziness; it is the fertile soil from which insight grows. Keep your curiosity alive. Play and experiment.
    \begin{itemize}
      \item \textbf{Idle Time:} Let your mind wander without goals or screens.
      \item \textbf{Side Quests:} Build things just for the joy of creating.
      \item \textbf{Protect Peace:} Recharge your spirit; rest is part of the work.
    \end{itemize}
  \end{posterbox}
\end{minipage}

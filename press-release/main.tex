% ── DATELINE + LEAD ─────────────────────────────────────────
{\bfseries SAN FRANCISCO, CA — July 5, 2026 —}
Nexora Technologies, Inc., the pioneer in AI-native cloud orchestration,
today announced \$120~million in Series~C financing led by Andreessen
Horowitz, with participation from Sequoia Capital and General Catalyst.
The capital funds expansion of the Nexora Orbit platform, global
go-to-market growth, and an applied-AI research division — bringing total
funding to \$215~million.

Nexora Orbit uses large language model reasoning and reinforcement learning
to continuously right-size, secure, and heal cloud infrastructure across
AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Customers including Meridian Financial,
RetailOS, and Apex Genomics have reduced infrastructure spend by an average
of 38\% while reaching 99.98\% uptime. Since launching its enterprise tier
in Q4~2024, Nexora has grown ARR to \$47~million (4$\times$ YoY) and
doubled headcount to 320 employees across San Francisco, New York, London,
and Singapore.

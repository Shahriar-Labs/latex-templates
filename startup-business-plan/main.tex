\section{\sectionicon{\faBolt}Executive Summary}

\begin{tcolorbox}[highlight]
  \textbf{Nexa Analytics} is an AI-driven business intelligence platform purpose-built for
  small and medium enterprises (SMEs). We translate raw operational data into actionable
  insights through an intuitive, no-code dashboard that requires zero data-science expertise.
  Founded in March 2024, we have already onboarded \textbf{47 paying customers} across four
  verticals and are growing monthly recurring revenue (MRR) at \textbf{28\% month-over-month}.
\end{tcolorbox}

\begin{multicols}{2}
\textbf{The Problem.} Over 78\% of SMEs collect operational data but lack the internal
resources to analyse it meaningfully. Off-the-shelf BI tools (Synapse BI, Vertex Dash)
are either too complex or priced for enterprise budgets, leaving a \textbf{\$18B
under-served market segment}.

\columnbreak

\textbf{Our Solution.} Nexa Analytics delivers enterprise-grade insight through a
subscription SaaS model starting at \$149/month. Our proprietary \textit{SmartLens}
AI engine surfaces anomalies, forecasts, and recommendations automatically --- no SQL,
no analyst required.
\end{multicols}
